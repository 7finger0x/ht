# Security

The detailed Hermes security control standard is in [docs/SecurityPolicy.md](docs/SecurityPolicy.md).

## Reporting a vulnerability

Report suspected vulnerabilities privately to **security@hermes-protocol.org**. Do not open a public issue for a vulnerability that could expose accounts, tenants, credentials, wallets, trading authority, personal information, or live execution.

Include:

- affected component and version;
- impact and prerequisites;
- reproducible steps or a minimal proof of concept;
- relevant request, event, or correlation identifiers with secrets removed;
- suggested mitigation, if known.

Do not include private keys, seed phrases, CEX secrets, bearer tokens, or unrelated personal data. Do not access another tenant, move assets, disrupt production, or perform social engineering.

**Before publication:** publish an encryption key and coordinated disclosure/safe-harbor policy, and state response targets and bounty terms if applicable.
